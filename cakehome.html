Welcome to Cake Website

Cake is the name given to various kinds of baked goods made from ingredients such as flour, butter, shortening, baking powder or eggs. Small cakes, tarts and other sweet baked goods are called "cake".

Cake may also refer to the dough from which such baked goods are made. Cake dough is rolled out thinly and used as a base for baked goods. Common cake dishes include pies, tarts and quiches.

Cake is distinguished from the bread by having a higher fat content, which contributes to a flaky or crumbly texture. A good cake is light and airy and fatty, but firm enough to support the weight of the filling. When making a shortcrust cake, care must be taken to blend the fat and flour thoroughly before adding.
